Vanilla Cupcake Recipe

  • Time: 28 mins
  • Serves: 24 Cupcakes
Ingredients Quantity
salted butter softened1/4 cup
sugar3/4 cup
milk 3/4 cup
large eggs2
vanilla extract2 teaspons
all-purpose flour1 3/4 cups
baking powder2 teaspoons

Step 1:
Line a muffin tin with cupcake liners. Preheat an oven to 350 degrees F.

Step 2:
In a large mixing bowl, use a hand mixer to cream the butter and sugar together until light and fluffy.

Step 3:
Add in milk, eggs, and vanilla extract and beat until combined.

Step 4:
Add in flour and baking powder and mix until just combined. Be sure to scrape the sides and bottom of the bowl to make sure everything gets mixed in.

Step 5:
Fill each cupcake liner half full. Bake at 350 for 14 to 18 minutes, until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

Step 6:
Remove cupcakes from pan and transfer to a wire rack to cool completely before frosting with your favorite frosting

Ice cream Recipe

  • Time: 2h 35mins
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
White sugar3/4 cup
Milk 2 1/4 cup
vanilla extract2 teaspons
Heavy whipping cream1 cup

Step 1:
Stir sugar, cream, and milk into a saucepan over low heat until sugar has dissolved. Heat just until mix is hot and a small ring of foam appears around the edge.

Step 2:
Transfer cream mixture to a pourable container such as a large measuring cup. Stir in vanilla extract, add coloring and chill mix thoroughly, at least 2 hours. (Overnight is best.) Pour cold ice cream mix into an ice cream maker, turn on the machine, and churn according to manufacturer's directions, 20 to 25 minutes.

Step 3:
When ice cream is softly frozen, serve immediately or place a piece of plastic wrap directly on the ice cream and place in freezer to ripen, 2 to 3 hours.

Chocolate recipe

  • Time: 20mins
  • Serves: 10
>
Ingredients Quantity
cocoa powder2 cup
sugar1/2 cup
flour1/4 teaspoon
Water1 cup
butter3/4 cup
milk2/3 cup
powdered sugar1/4 cup

Step 1:
Place cocoa and butter in processor and mix until they form a paste. Now, Fill the pan about 1/4 full with water and then place the bowl on top of the water.

Step 2:
Now, put the chocolate paste in the bowl and then heat until the mixture is as hot as needed. Put the mixture back in the processor and mix till smooth.

Step 3:
Then warm the milk to room temperature. Add sugar, flour and milk to the paste and mix the batter well so that no lumps are formed.

Step 4:
Now pour the mixture into molds and place it in the fridge till they become hard. Take the pieces out and munch away
